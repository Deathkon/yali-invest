Our Solution: ALTA

ALTA is a suite of AI models and enabling tools for African languages. We pioneered with Kinyarwanda, creating our flagship products: the ALTA Model (a foundational Kinyarwanda LLM in active development) and the ALTA Tokenizer (launched March 2025).
